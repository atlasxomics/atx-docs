## Overview

Interactive [Latch Plots](https://latch.bio/) apps for visualizing epigenomics outputs. Source: [`plots/plots_epigenome`](https://github.com/atlasxomics/plots_epigenome). Available plots include: heatmap, violin plots, volcano, motif logo, proportion plot, track browser, neighborhood analysis, bulk score genes, and compare.
